ACT Science Center

Palakol, West Godavari District, Andhra Pradesh

Joint Initiative by:

Vidya Chaitanya Samakya, Palakol; Allamraju Charitable Trust (ACT), Hyderabad; Kriya Society, Kakinada

ACT Science Center aims to complement formal school education by providing a practical learning environment beyond the classroom, enriching students through hands-on scientific experiments that foster curiosity and conceptual understanding. The center cultivates a spirit of enquiry, creativity, and scientific temperament through interactive activity-based learning, while promoting application-oriented research and skill development among school and college students. Through seminars, guest talks, and competitions in Science and Mathematics, we nurture interest and confidence. Additionally, the center provides training and workshops for teachers to integrate experimental and experiential learning in classrooms, creates awareness about diverse scientific fields and emerging technologies through regular sessions and lectures, and inspires young innovators by connecting them with mentors, scientists, and educational institutions.
